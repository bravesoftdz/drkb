Дата в SQL запросах в MS Access

01.01.2010

Для дат используйте #:

SELECT Students.Name,Students.BirthDate,Teachers.Name 
FROM Teachers 
INNER JOIN Students ON Students.TeacherID=Teachers.ID 
WHERE Students.BirthDate BETWEEN #09/02/87# AND #01/01/00#
