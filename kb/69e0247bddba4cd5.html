Управление транзакциями

01.01.2010

Each function listed below begins, ends, or inquires about the status of a transaction.

DbiBeginTran:

Begins a transaction.

DbiEndTran:

Ends a transaction.

DbiGetTranInfo:

Retrieves the transaction state.
